# Historical Timeline

## Pre-History: The Collapse of Traditional Governance (2025-2035)

### 2025-2027: The Legitimacy Crisis
- Multiple democratic governments fail to handle climate/migration crises
- Regulatory capture becomes undeniable
- Public trust in institutions collapses globally
- **Damon's Insight**: Law without enforcers is impossible; enforcers without law are tyrants

### 2028-2030: The Prototype Years  
- Damon develops blockchain governance for corporate mergers
- Success rate approaches 100% vs. 60% for traditional courts
- Early adopters: Estonia, Singapore, Rwanda
- **Key Innovation**: Self-executing contracts with oracle integration

### 2031-2035: The Great Migration
- Nation-states begin adopting blockchain law systems
- Traditional legal apparatus becomes redundant
- Lawyers retrain as code auditors or become obsolete
- **Damon's Position**: Chief architect of the new world order

## The Blockchain Hegemony (2035-2045)

### 2035-2040: Consolidation
- Damon's protocols become global standard
- Universal property registry established
- Smart contracts govern 80% of commercial activity
- **The Trap**: All systems route through Damon-controlled infrastructure

### 2040-2042: The Rent-Seeking Architecture
- Micro-fees on every transaction
- Licensing requirements for contract modifications
- Patent locks on core governance protocols
- **Public Perception**: Small price for perfect law

### 2043-2045: Peak Damon
- Controls ~40% of global economic activity through passive ownership
- Hailed as architect of post-scarcity economics
- Announces immortality project as "next step in human governance"
- **Kaelen's Position**: Heir apparent and trusted lieutenant
