**Length**: ~1800 words

## Scene Integration Notes

### Vignette Placement Strategy
- **Act I**: No family vignettes (world still under Damon's order)
- **Act II**: Regular interspersion showing gradual improvement
- **Act III**: Climactic vignettes showing complete transformation
- **Coda**: Final vignette representing liberated future

### Pacing Architecture  
- **Damon scenes**: Declining power arc, increasing desperation
- **Family scenes**: Ascending hope arc, material improvement
- **Media scenes**: Growing awareness arc, moral complexity
- **System scenes**: Technical exposition, philosophical framework

### Character Development Threads
- **Damon**: God-king → Investigator → Ghost → Tragic Recognition
- **Kaelen**: Hidden presence felt through consequences and speculation
- **World**: Oppressed abundance → Gradual liberation → Transformed society
- **Reader**: Initial sympathy for Damon → Growing understanding of systemic issues → Moral ambiguity

### Stephenson Integration Points
Each scene includes opportunities for:
- **Technical digressions**: How systems work and fail
- **Historical parallels**: Similar events throughout history  
- **Philosophical exploration**: Deep questions about power, justice, technology
- **Economic analysis**: How wealth flows and accumulates
- **Cultural observation**: Human behavior in systemic context
